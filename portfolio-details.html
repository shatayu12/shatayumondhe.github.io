About the Project

WCWH is a UT health and wellbeing research initiative, seeking to include communities traditionally left out of the research process. Hornsense is an app used by families to consolidate data from their Fitbit bands and environmental sensors. The data is presented to them so they can make informed decisions regarding their health.
